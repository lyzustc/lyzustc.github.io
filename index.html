Short Biography

I received my B.E. in Automation in 2019 from University of Science and Technology of China. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University. My advisor is Dr. Shuiwang Ji.

I am actively looking for full-time applied scientist/research scientist/data scientist/machine learning engineer positions in industry. Feel free to contact me if you would like to offer good opportunities!
